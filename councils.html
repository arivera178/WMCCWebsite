Councils

Councils are an important part of the chapter! It is a way of members to take part in the planning and creation of events in the chapter! Each council focuses on a different topic. Each council consists of 2 Senior Officers, 2 Junior Officers and the selected Council Staff. Council staff are carry out and lead the councils alongside the council leads! Each council provides community service time to the staff and the occacional members! It is the easiest way for students to obtain those required Community Service Hours! Talk to the council leads to see what the councils are up to and join one tody as a member or apply to become staff!
